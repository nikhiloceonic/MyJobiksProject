FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the validity of this service?

Recruiter Connection service is valid for 6 months from the date of purchase. After 6 months, your credits will expire

What is a credit? How many credits will I get if I buy Recruiter Connection

In Jobiks, 1 credit =1 message. You can purchase either 5, 10 or 20 credits depending on the subscription you choose.

What happens if a recruiter does not reply back to my message?

In case a recruiter does not take any action on your message within 15 days, you will get a credit back. Please note that credit back happens only once and the same credits can be used for another recruiter

Is there a limit on the number of messages I can send once I purchase the service?

You can connect with a maximum of 30 recruiters in a month. You cannot send a message again to same recruiters for 30 days.

Can I use this service to connect with international recruiters?

Yes, you can send messages to international recruiters. There are 5000+ recruiters for international roles on Jobiks
